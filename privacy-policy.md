# BodyPhase Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2026**

## Data collection

BodyPhase does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with any server, third party, or developer.

## What is stored and where

All data you enter — body weight, workout logs, habit completions, and profile settings — is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage (UserDefaults).

Optionally, data syncs to Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store if you are signed into iCloud on your device. This sync happens between your own devices only. The developer has no access to your iCloud data.

## Third-party SDKs and services

BodyPhase uses no analytics, advertising, crash reporting, tracking, or third-party SDKs of any kind. There are no external network requests.

## Local notifications

The app schedules local notifications on your device to remind you of meals and workout sessions. These notifications are processed entirely on-device. No notification content is sent to any server.
